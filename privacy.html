Privacy Policy

This policy explains how handles project inquiries, contact details, and website interaction information.

Information we collect

We may collect name, phone number, email address, service address or service area, project details, preferred timing, photos or files you choose to submit, and communication records related to your inquiry.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, understand project scope, route requests to appropriate service categories, connect homeowners with independent local providers, improve website performance, and maintain business records.

Sharing with service partners

When you request project matching, we may share relevant inquiry details with independent remodeling providers so they can evaluate availability and contact you. Those providers operate under their own contracts, licenses, insurance, and privacy practices.

Retention and choices

You may contact us to request updates or deletion of inquiry information where legally and operationally possible. Some records may be retained for compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes.
