Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit our website or use our project intake and contractor connection services.

1. Introduction

("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information collected through this website, contact forms, project request tools, communications, analytics, and related online services.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use the website or submit project information.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us. We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out contact forms, request project matching, ask for bathroom remodeling information, communicate with us, participate in surveys, or otherwise contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address or service area, project details, preferred timing, budget context, photos or files you choose to submit, and any other information you provide.

Automatically collected information. When you visit our website, we may automatically collect information about your device and browsing activity, such as IP address, approximate location data, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website addresses, device information, and interaction data.

Cookies and tracking technologies. We use cookies, pixels, web beacons, and similar technologies to support website functionality, analytics, performance measurement, form flows, and marketing measurement. For more information, please review our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use information to respond to inquiries, understand project scope, route requests to appropriate service categories, connect homeowners with independent local service providers, communicate about project requests, improve website content, analyze performance, maintain business records, prevent fraud or misuse, comply with legal obligations, and support marketing or follow-up communications where permitted by law.

We do not use your information to perform remodeling work ourselves. Our use of project information is tied to website operation, project intake, service matching, referral coordination, and business administration.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense of exchanging it for money. We may share information with independent local service providers when you request project matching or ask to be contacted about bathroom remodeling services. These providers may use your information to evaluate availability, contact you, schedule consultations, provide estimates, or discuss project options.

We may also share information with website hosting providers, analytics providers, advertising or measurement platforms, form tools, customer communication tools, security vendors, professional advisors, or other service providers that help us operate the website and business. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, in connection with a business transfer, or with your consent.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
